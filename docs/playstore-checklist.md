# Food Hungry Play Store Checklist

## Required

- Google Play Console account.
- US$25 one-time registration fee.
- App name: Food Hungry.
- Android package name: `com.foodhungry.app`.
- App icon.
- Feature graphic.
- Screenshots.
- Privacy policy URL.
- Data safety form.
- App content rating questionnaire.
- Closed testing track if required for your account type.
- Signed Android App Bundle `.aab`.

## Later Before Production

- Real database.
- Login and authentication.
- Payment flow.
- Order cancellation/refund policy.
- Restaurant onboarding rules.
- Customer support contact.
- Terms and conditions.
